# Ableton Live Timeline

- `fn←`: Move the playhead to beginning of track
- `⌥␣`: Play selection
- `F9`: Record

## Mouse

- `⌘Scroll`: Zoom

## Playback

- `⌘L`: Toggle loop
- `␣`: Play from the insert marker (click to place the insert marker anywhere)
- `⇧␣`: Pause / play (e.g., without starting again from the insert marker)
